About Suburban Lawnscapes | West Orange, NJ

Suburban Lawnscapes believes in not just cutting and cleaning up lawns and landscapes we also believe in caring for them as if they were our own. Lending over 5 years of combined experience in landscape maintenance and lawn care to every property we manage, whether large, small, commercial or residential, the Suburban Lawnscapes team is able to put our passion and credentials to work.

Lawn care and landscape maintenance executed to meet or exceed the highest standards

Lawns as green and gorgeous as they are healthy call for your landscape contractor to have more than a basic understanding of grasses typical to northern New Jersey. Your lawn care specialists at Suburban Lawnscapes have a familiarity with the region's planting zones, soils, insects and pests, and variations of turf.


Our customers deserve the best treatment, their lawns and landscapes the best care. That means customized lawn and landscape maintenance packages at prices that suit individual budgets and each landscape's unique requirements.

Receive comprehensive lawn care and landscape maintenance from an experienced team. Call (973)380-4132 or contact Suburban Lawnscapes online.
